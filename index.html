Hisham Abdulrahman Al Delawar

About Hisham

Hisham Abdulrahman Al Delawar is a Bahraini investment analyst, entrepreneur, and a registered padel player in the national rankings. He currently serves as an Analyst in the Investment Department at Osool Asset Management, leveraging his academic background in accounting and finance alongside a strong interest in investment strategies and the integration of AI in financial operations.

Hisham is deeply devoted to his family, especially his parents, who he considers the foundation of his success and the most important part of his life.

Hisham completed his schooling at Ibn Khuldoon National School, one of Bahrain’s leading private institutions, where he also developed his leadership and athletic skills.

Formula 1 Career & Media Features

Hisham has also built a remarkable career behind the scenes in motorsport. For the past six years, he has worked as a scutineer in the FIA Formula 1 World Championship, contributing to the technical inspection and regulation enforcement that ensure fair and safe racing.

His experience and presence in the paddock have also led to appearances in the Netflix series "Drive to Survive", offering fans a behind-the-scenes glimpse into his role. Hisham's work was even featured in national newspapers, highlighting his growing profile in both motorsport and public media. His story was covered in Akhbar Al Khaleej, a prominent Bahraini news outlet.

Based on his Instagram stories, Hisham has developed a close friendship with several Formula 1 and Formula 2 drivers over the years. Notably, drivers like Richard Verschoor and Ollie Goethe have shared moments with Hisham on social media, including pictures of them playing padel together. These connections reflect Hisham’s growing influence and presence within the motorsport community beyond the paddock.
